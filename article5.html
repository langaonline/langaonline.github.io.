Chinga, Sjoko, Sdumo


When it comes to elicala lomcula ka Langa we always boasting about the late Brenda Fassie. Either than her we have never had other promising artist besides Fosta, naye he did not blow up kahulu but ebhe right. However there has been a change, ngoku who do have a few good artist, who with a good fan base can be recognize by good producers and kick start good careers in music. The likes of Sdumo, Chinga and Sjoka are excellent artist in their respective fields to name few.

Sdumo the energetic kwaito artist shook us with his hit song “starter motor” which became the song in everyone’s lips for some time. As we all know every hit song has its time period. Once that period came to an end, as fans Sdumo kange asinike enye ngoma echisayo, but we still waiting patiently for it. Let’s look out for him in the next coming months.

A dj and a rapper Sjoko has been the name in many young girls jaws with the hit sing “zifuna ntoni girls”. He is known for his skills on the decks and how he sets the crowd alive. He is constantly doing gigs around Cape Town and organizing events for local artist. His latest event was in Guga Stebe which featured Baseline and Aux womdantso amongst many incredible artist. Sadly the lineup did not feature many of our artist. I wonder why?

Chinga a rapper in a league of his own many would say. Known for his energy on stage and how he drops hot bars on beats. It is rumored that he single handedly took on the IMK group in a street battle which he won. After that street battle IMK were now to be found. He recently created a music video for his latest single “bbbb”. He is also known for the song “bn”. Where he addresses life wase ekasi as a rapper. Watch out for him, more is coming from this dude.

We have excellent artist apha kwa Langa lets support them and create hype around them. *lets support our artist*
